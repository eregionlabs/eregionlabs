Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Bet You Won't?

After downloading the app, open it and follow the on-screen instructions to create your profile. You'll be ready to play with friends in just a few taps.

Is Bet You Won't free to use?

Bet You Won't is free to download and play. Some optional in-app features may be available for purchase.

What devices are supported?

Bet You Won't is available on iPhone running iOS 17.0 or later.

The app is crashing or not loading. What should I do?

Try these steps to resolve the issue:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed from the App Store.
  2. Restart the app by closing it completely and reopening it.
  3. Restart your device.
  4. If the issue persists, contact us at info@eregionlabs.com with details about the problem.

How do I report inappropriate content?

We take content moderation seriously. If you encounter any inappropriate content or behavior, please report it immediately by emailing info@eregionlabs.com with details and screenshots if possible.

How do I delete my account?

To request account deletion, please email info@eregionlabs.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and delete all associated data within 30 days.

I'm having trouble with notifications. How do I fix this?

Make sure notifications are enabled for Bet You Won't in your device settings. Go to Settings > Notifications > Bet You Won't and ensure that "Allow Notifications" is turned on.

How do I request a refund for an in-app purchase?

In-app purchases are processed through Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID to submit your request.
